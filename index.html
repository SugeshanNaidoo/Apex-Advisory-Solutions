About Apex Advisory

Leading business transformation experts with deep roots in South Africa

Our Story

Excellence in business restructuring since our inception

Business Restructuring in South Africa

Apex Advisory Solutions is an emerging professional services firm dedicated to guiding South African businesses through the complexities of financial restructuring, regulatory compliance, and strategic growth. As a newly established advisory, we combine legal precision with financial acumen to help clients build stronger, more resilient enterprises in an evolving economic landscape. Our approach is rooted in collaboration, innovation, and a deep understanding of both the regulatory and commercial environments that shape modern business in South Africa.

Our South African Expertise

Founded by Avathar Soni Naidoo and Ashendran Naidoo, Apex Advisory Solutions brings together a powerful blend of legal and financial expertise. With Avathar’s extensive background as an admitted attorney of the High Court and Ashendran’s credentials as a Chartered Business Accountant and Tax Practitioner, the firm offers clients a comprehensive and trusted advisory experience. Supported by a network of strategic partners. Apex Advisory Solutions is positioned to deliver holistic, integrated solutions that empower businesses to adapt, thrive, and lead in a competitive marketplace.

Our Founders

Visionary leaders driving business transformation across South Africa

Avathar Soni Naidoo

Co-Founder & Director

Admitted Attorney Of The High Court Of South Africa

Avathar was admitted as an attorney of the High Court and brings extensive expertise across a range of legal disciplines. His background includes civil litigation, legal consulting, and the management of high-volume legal operations. He has also supported tech-driven startups and advised SMEs navigating compliance in an increasingly digitized business landscape. Avathar is recognized for combining strong technical legal knowledge with a practical, solutions-oriented approach. He is committed to delivering client-focused counsel that fosters resilience and long-term success.

Ashendran Naidoo

Co-Founder & Director

CBA(SA), AT(SA), TP(SA), Commissioner of Oaths(RSA)

Ashendran is a Chartered Business Accountant with CIBA, a registered Tax Practitioner with SAIT, and affiliated with SAICA. With deep expertise in accounting and taxation, he has helped individuals and businesses achieve financial clarity and sustainable growth. Known for delivering precise and client-focused solutions, Ashendran blends technical proficiency with a strong sense of professionalism and service excellence. His work reflects a commitment to supporting clients in building robust financial strategies for continued success.
